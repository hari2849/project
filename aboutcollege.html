Dr.D.K.Audikesavulu, Founder Chairman

Sreenivasa Institute of Technology and Management Studies (SITAMS) was established in the year 1998-1999 under the Sreenivasa Trust.The founder Chairman of the trust is Late Dr.D.K.Audikesavulu, Former Chairman of Tirumala Tirupati Devastanams. He is a Mechanical Engineer by profession and also a prominent industrialist having industrial establishments in the states of Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh

Smt.D.A.Sathya Prabha, Chair Person

Smt.D.A.Sathya Prabha, M.L.A.,Chittoor (W/o Late Sri.D.K. Audikesavulu, Ex.M.P.,Loksabha), is the present Managing Trustee, Srinivasa Trust and the Chairperson of the Sreenivasa Institute of Technology and Management Studies (SITAMS). Smt. Sathya Prabha, as a Member of the Srinivasa Trust is actively involved in all Trust activities along with her husband, throughout her life.

Sri.K.Ranganadham, Executive Vice-Chairman

The Executive Vice-Chairman of SITAMS is Sri.K.Ranganadham. He was a Former Chairman and Managing Director, Andhra Pradesh Southern Power Distribution Company Limited. A.P, Special Officer (Appellate Tribunal for Electricity) for NESCO, WESCO & SOUTHCO of the State of ORISSA.

Dr. P. Ramesh Kumar, Principal

Dr. P. Ramesh Kumar obtained his BE (Mech. Engg.) from Andhra University, Vishakapatnam during 1991. He did his ME in Thermal Power Engineering from Annamalai University, Annamalai Nagar during 1993 and obtained his Ph.D from S.V.University, Tirupati during 2008. He is having more than 22 years of Teaching experience in different Engineering Colleges at various levels and 9 years as Principal. He published 10 papers at National and International level journals and Conferences. He is a life member in Indian Society for Technical Education and Fellow of Institute of Engineers (India). He also served as a member, Board of studies of Mechanical Engineering for Yogi Vemana University, Kadapa and Doctoral Committee Member for Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Viswa Mahavidyalaya, Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu.
